Pickup & Delivery

  • Included: up to 10 miles one-way (20 miles round trip).
  • Beyond that: $1.50 per mile (charged on the extra miles beyond the included range).
  • Equivalent of about $0.725 per mile when calculating total miles driven.

Tip: If you want the labor discount, self drop-off / self pick-up is still the cheapest route.

Bench Fees

  • Amplifiers: $35 bench fee (applied toward labor if you proceed; otherwise diagnostic fee).
  • All other instruments & services (incl. small electronics): $25 bench fee (same policy).
