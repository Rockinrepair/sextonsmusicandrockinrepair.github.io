About

I’m Greg (SMRR). I focus on clean, reliable repairs that make your instrument or rig feel right again — from setups and fretwork to electronics, pedals, and amps.

I serve Georgetown, Lexington, and Winchester, KY. Pickup & delivery is available by request.

What I work on

  • Guitars & basses: setups, fret ends, electronics, hardware swaps
  • Acoustics: pickups, bone nuts, tuner swaps, action/intonation work
  • Pedals & pedalboards: repair, troubleshooting, wiring cleanup
  • Amps: diagnostics, maintenance, repairs (tube & solid-state)
  • Small electronics (non-phone): consoles, sound bars, radios, etc.
