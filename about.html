Get to Know Sexton’s

I’m Greg Sexton — founder, tech, and the guy who gets you back to Rockin Fast.

Sexton’s Music and Rockin Repair LLC was built on one simple idea: you shouldn’t have to wait forever or sell a kidney to get your gear back in shape. Whether it’s a worn-out fretboard, a buzzing amp, or electronics that’ve seen better days — I’ve got the tools, the skills, and the passion to bring it back to life.

With over a decade of hands-on experience and deep roots in the Kentucky music scene, this shop isn’t just a service — it’s part of the community. No frills, no fluff — just solid, honest work. Let’s keep your sound alive.
